# 📊 Sales Trend Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

The Sales Trend Analysis Dashboard is an interactive Power BI project designed to analyze sales performance, identify trends, and provide actionable business insights. The dashboard helps track key metrics such as sales revenue, profit, orders, and customer performance across different time periods.

---

## 🎯 Objective

- Analyze sales trends over time.
- Monitor key business KPIs.
- Identify top-performing products and regions.
- Track revenue and profit growth.
- Support data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🛠️ Tools Used

- Power BI
- Power Query
- DAX
- Data Modeling
- Data Visualization

---

## 📈 Key Features

- Interactive Sales Dashboard
- Revenue and Profit Analysis
- Monthly and Yearly Sales Trends
- Top Products Performance
- Regional Sales Analysis
- KPI Tracking and Monitoring
- Dynamic Filters and Slicers

---

## 📊 Dashboard Insights

- Identified peak sales periods and seasonal trends.
- Analyzed revenue and profit performance.
- Evaluated product-wise sales contribution.
- Compared regional sales performance.
- Tracked business growth through KPI metrics.
